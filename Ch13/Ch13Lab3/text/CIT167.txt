Routing & Switching Essentials

 Covers the architecture components, and operations of routers and switches in a small network.  Helps students learn how to configure a router and a switch for basic functionality.  Helps students configure and troubleshoot routers and switches and resolve common issues with RIPv1, RIPv2, single-area and multi-area OSPF, virtual LANs, and inter-VLAN routing in both IPv4 and IPv6 networks.  Prerequisite:  CIT 161 OR Consent of Instructor
